# I’d Love to Say Hello and Have a Chat

Whether it’s for a [bespoke illustration]({% link commissions.md %}) to celebrate a special moment, a workshop or art class enquiry, or just to find out more about live illustration – I’m always here with a smile and happy to help.

Please do get in touch if you’d like me to run a workshop where you are. I have experience in a wide range of techniques and projects, ranging from watercolour to collagraph, sketchbook techniques to sculptural and textural painting, clay to collage. 

Workshops can be a great way to bring friends, family, businesses, or colleagues together, and
I’ll bring bucketfuls of enthusiasm and top tips on the day.

Contact me via email

**Email: nicolourok@gmail.com**
